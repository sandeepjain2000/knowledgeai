Unique Schools offers four core digital solutions: School Communication App, Digital Sign-In/Out System, Unique Pay payment platform, and Website Development services…

1.2s

The Digital Sign-In/Out system handles visitor management through a tablet-based kiosk placed at school reception. When a visitor arrives, they check in by entering their name, contact number, and the purpose of their visit — optionally scanning a QR code pre-sent via the school app.

The following data is captured and stored per visit: full name, contact number, time in / time out, host teacher or staff member, and visit reason. All data is encrypted at rest and hosted on GDPR-compliant EU servers. Visitor records are automatically purged after 90 days unless a school administrator extends the retention window.

For contractor and staff sign-in, the system also supports badge printing and can trigger an SMS notification to the relevant staff member on arrival.
