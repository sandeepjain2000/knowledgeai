US SCHOOL SHOP - FEES
Unique Pay payments system is integrated with STRIPE payments gateway. The costs associated with  Unique Pay payments system are as follows:
1) Unique Schools once off set up fee €495 + vat. This includes one in school training session of 2 hours.
2) Unique Schools Annual Fee which is charged at €3.00 + vat per student per annum regardless of number  or value of transactions (subject to minimum annual fee of €695).
3) Merchant banking fees from STRIPE. There are two elements to the merchant fee which is charged by  Stripe on transactions. The first is a percentage of the transaction value and the second element is a basic  transaction fee which is charged in addition and is per transaction regardless of value.
