| Q  A parent has emailed asking the school a general question. What do we do? | A  Inform the parent this email is for App technical issues only. Direct them to use the Parent Note option in the App, or contact the school directly via email/phone. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent wants to submit an absence, late note, or permission-to-leave note via email. How do we handle this? | A  Attendance notes cannot be accepted by this support address. Ask the parent to submit the note through the School App or contact the school office directly. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent says their VSWare username is not appearing on the login page. What should we advise? | A  Unique Schools does not hold VSWare usernames. Advise the parent to contact their school office and request that VSWare send the username directly via SMS through the VSWare platform. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent says they are locked out of their VSWare account. What is the resolution? | A  This is a VSWare-managed issue. Direct the parent to contact their school office and ask them to unlock the VSWare account. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent has not received their VSWare OTP verification code. What should we tell them? | A  Advise the parent that OTP codes can take up to 24 hours to arrive. If still not received after 24 hours, they should contact the school, who will then follow up with VSWare directly. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent sees a blank screen after logging into VSWare. What is the fix? | A  Ask them to click the VSWare icon in the upper left corner of the screen. This refreshes VSWare and should display the student list. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent has children in different schools and wants to access both VSWare accounts. How does this work? | A  Each student will appear in the relevant school's App. The parent cannot use one school's VSWare login to access another school's system. They must use the App for each respective school. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent is asking about Tyro login credentials. Can we help? | A  Tyro is a separate system — it is only a link within the App. We do not have access to it. Direct the parent to contact the school or Tyro directly. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent gets the message 'The mobile number you are trying to register with could not be found in the schools database.' What does this mean and how do we resolve it? | A  The mobile number used is not on the school's record. Advise the parent to contact the school office and request they update the mobile number in the Unique Schools App system to the one they are registering with. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent receives the message 'The mobile number is already taken' during registration. What should we advise? | A  This means the parent has already registered via another school app. They do not need to register again. Advise them to log in using their existing email and password. If forgotten, use the Forgot Password option. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent receives 'The email address is already registered' during sign-up. What is the next step? | A  The email was registered previously, possibly for another school. Advise the parent not to register again and to log in with their existing email and password. If forgotten, use the Forgot Password option. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent wants to change their registered email address. Can we do this for them? | A  We cannot change email addresses on behalf of parents. Instruct them to log in with their current email, tap their profile name, and select 'Change Email ID' from their profile settings. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent wants to change their registered phone number. What is the process? | A  The parent must first contact the school office to update the number in the Unique Schools App system. Once confirmed, they go to their Profile > 'Change Contact Number' in the App. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent has forgotten their App password. What do we advise? | A  Direct the parent to use the 'Forgot Password' option on the App's login screen to reset their password. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent has forgotten their 4-digit PIN for the App. How can they reset or remove it? | A  Instruct them to: tap their name in the Side Menu > scroll to 'Change 4 digit pin code' > choose Disable PIN to remove it, or enter a new PIN and confirm with their password, then press Submit. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent received an email saying their instalment payment failed. What should we tell them? | A  The payment was declined by their bank (possibly due to card expiry). Our system will automatically retry 3 more times over the next 5 business days. If no action is needed and retries succeed, the parent does not need to do anything. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent is having trouble adding multiple items to their payment basket. What is the correct process? | A  Advise the parent to click directly on the 'Shop' menu in the sidebar (not via the Attention Please dashboard). From there they can select items one by one, adding each to the cart, then check out all items together. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent says they cannot see the expected items in the shop. What do we do? | A  The school may not have added the parent's profile to the relevant shop item. Direct the parent to contact the school and ask them to add their profile. They can then refresh the App to view the items. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent wants an item removed from their shop list. Can we help? | A  We cannot remove items on their behalf. The parent should contact the school directly and ask them to remove their profile from that shop item. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent has a query about EasyPayments. Can we resolve it? | A  EasyPayments is a third-party system accessible only as a link within the App. We do not have access to it. Direct the parent to contact EasyPayments or the school directly for assistance. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent reports that their name or their child's name is spelled incorrectly in the App. How is this corrected? | A  Unique Schools cannot edit school data directly. The parent must contact the school office and ask them to correct the spelling in the student record in the Unique Schools App system. |
| --- | --- |
| Q  A parent says not all of their children appear in the App, even though they all attend the school. What should we advise? | A  Direct the parent to contact the school office and ask them to check and update the student records in the Unique Schools App system to ensure all children are linked to the parent's profile. |
